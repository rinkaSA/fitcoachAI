# Bodyweight exercises

## Push-up
Primary muscles: chest, shoulders, triceps, core.
Use when: upper-body push training is appropriate.
Regression: incline push-up or knee push-up.

## Pull-up
Primary muscles: lats, upper back, biceps.
Use when: upper-body pull training is appropriate.
Regression: assisted pull-up, band-assisted pull-up, or negative pull-up.

## Bodyweight squat
Primary muscles: quads, glutes.
Use when: lower-body training is appropriate.
Avoid high volume when quads or glutes are sore.

## Plank
Primary muscles: core.
Use when: core stability is appropriate.
Regression: shorter holds.

## Dead bug
Primary muscles: core.
Use when: beginner-friendly core training is appropriate.
