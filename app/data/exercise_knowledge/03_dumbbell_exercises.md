# Dumbbell exercises

## Dumbbell goblet squat
Primary muscles: quads, glutes, core.
Equipment: one dumbbell.
Use when: lower-body training is appropriate.
Avoid or reduce when: quads or glutes are very sore.

## Dumbbell Romanian deadlift
Primary muscles: hamstrings, glutes, lower back.
Equipment: two dumbbells.
Use when: posterior-chain training is appropriate.
Avoid heavy loading if hamstrings or glutes are very sore.

## One-arm dumbbell row
Primary muscles: lats, upper back, biceps.
Equipment: one dumbbell.
Use when: upper-body pull training is appropriate.

## Dumbbell bench press
Primary muscles: chest, shoulders, triceps.
Equipment: two dumbbells and bench or floor.
Use when: upper-body push training is appropriate.

## Dumbbell shoulder press
Primary muscles: shoulders, triceps.
Equipment: two dumbbells.
Use when: upper-body push training is appropriate.
